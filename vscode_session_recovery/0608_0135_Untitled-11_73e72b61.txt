Case-wise coverage (do not typeset)
CASE 1 = topmost forwarder, NO anchor above (only L1).
  1a) L1 skip, stays skipped (in flight)
  1b) L1 skip, then NOT skip (repeat allocs
      MSHR at L1, merges below)
  1c) L1 skip, resolved by HIT at L2
CASE 2 = no MSHR here, anchor ABOVE.
  2a) L2 skip   (anchor above = L1)
  2b) LLC skip  (anchor above = L2)
  2c) L2 skip + LLC HIT  *** NOT YET WRITTEN ***
CASE 3 = holds standard MSHR (the anchor). Any level, by skip-bit of level ABOVE:
  3a) upper bit CLEAR (upper kept, or repeat-cleared per 1b)
  3b) upper bit SET   (upper skipped)
NOTE: clearing of a skip bit happens in CASE 1 (1b); CASE 3 only READS it. cross-ref.
